# Home Page Copy Rewrite Plan

## The Problem

The user has identified four specific descriptions that read as internal design commentary rather than customer-facing marketing copy:

1. **features-grid.tsx**: "The homepage now shifts from static marketing blocks to high-trust service signals, using the same cinematic language introduced in the hero."
2. **popular-routes.tsx**: "A destination gallery built around contrast, motion, and strong hierarchy so route discovery feels premium rather than utilitarian."
3. **process-flow.tsx**: "The process section is intentionally minimal so it acts as a visual reset between richer destination and fleet content."
4. **fleet-preview.tsx**: "The fleet preview borrows the tone of the hire catalog while simplifying it into a marketing showcase with comfort-led storytelling."

---

## Targeted Fixes for Each File

### 1. features-grid.tsx (Line ~57)

**Current:**

> "The homepage now shifts from static marketing blocks to high-trust service signals, using the same cinematic language introduced in the hero."

**Proposed replacement:**

> "From real-time tracking to professional drivers, every service is built to make your journey smooth and reliable."

### 2. popular-routes.tsx (Line ~41)

**Current:**

> "A destination gallery built around contrast, motion, and strong hierarchy so route discovery feels premium rather than utilitarian."

**Proposed replacement:**

> "Discover Nigeria's most traveled routes. Direct buses, reliable schedules, and comfortable coaches to get you there."

### 3. process-flow.tsx (Line ~38)

**Current:**

> "The process section is intentionally minimal so it acts as a visual reset between richer destination and fleet content."

**Proposed replacement:**

> "Booking your bus is simple. Find your route, choose your seat, and you're ready to go."

### 4. fleet-preview.tsx (Line ~50)

**Current:**

> "The fleet preview borrows the tone of the hire catalog while simplifying it into a marketing showcase with comfort-led storytelling."

**Proposed replacement:**

> "Travel in comfort. Our fleet features modern coaches with AC, WiFi, and spacious seating for a premium journey."
